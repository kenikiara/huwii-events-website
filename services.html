Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge for team building services in Kenya?

There are no fixed rates for our team building packages. Pricing depends on the number of participants, event location, duration and the specific services or activities required. Whether it's a corporate retreat, school fun day or church group outing, we provide customized packages to suit your goals and budget. Contact us for a personalized quote.

What types of team building activities do you offer?

We offer a wide range of custom team building activities in Kenya, including outdoor games, group challenges, corporate Olympics, icebreakers and obstacle courses. We cater to corporate teams, executives, schools, churches and families — all designed to boost collaboration, communication and team morale.

Do you organize corporate and executive retreats?

Yes! We offer expertly curated corporate retreats and executive team building retreats in Kenya, held in premium venues with strategic workshops, leadership training and relaxing team bonding experiences.

Do you provide transport for team building events?

Absolutely. We provide reliable transport solutions — from corporate shuttle services to school buses and group travel coordination — so all participants arrive safely and on time, no matter the event location.

Do you supply MCs, facilitators and event setup?

Yes. Huwii Events is a full-service events company offering professional facilitators, MCs, motivational speakers and event coordination — plus sound systems, tents, décor and branding for a smooth, successful event.

Can you plan traditional events like Ruracio?

Yes, we're experienced in organizing traditional Kenyan events such as Ruracio ceremonies. We respect cultural practices while providing modern support like décor, catering coordination and MC services.

How do I book an event with Huwii Events?

Booking is easy! Reach out through our contact form, phone or email and we'll schedule a free consultation, recommend activities and support you from venue sourcing to facilitation.
