Games FAQ

Is delivery free?

Delivery is free within selected areas of Nairobi. A small delivery fee applies for locations outside Nairobi or for larger items like bouncing castles and trampolines. All delivery costs are confirmed before booking.

Which areas in Kenya do you serve?

We serve Nairobi and surrounding areas, plus major towns across Kenya. From Mombasa to Nakuru, we provide game rentals and event equipment nationwide.

How much does it cost to hire games for an event?

Pricing depends on game types, event duration and location. We offer competitive rates for board games, outdoor games and giant games. Contact us for a tailored quote.

How long does delivery take?

We offer same-day delivery within Nairobi and nearby areas. For large items like giant slides or trampolines, delivery typically takes 2–5 days. To ensure availability, we recommend booking early for popular items.

Do you provide staff to run the games at the event?

Yes! Our facilitators lead team building activities and ensure smooth event flow. We handle everything from game supervision to interactive activities for a fun and memorable experience.

How much is facilitation?

Facilitation prices depend on your event's size and duration. Whether it's a small gathering or a large corporate event, we offer affordable, customized packages for game facilitation.

Do you have games for kids?

Yes, we offer a variety of kids' games, including interactive kids' games, board games and outdoor play activities designed to keep children entertained and engaged in a safe environment.

Do you have sports equipment?

We offer sports equipment for hire, including football gear, volleyball sets and fitness equipment. Perfect for school sports days, corporate events or tournaments.

What else do you have for hire besides games?

Besides games, we offer bouncing castles, trampolines, giant slides and inflatable obstacle courses — perfect for birthdays, corporate events or any celebration.

Can you take people to places they can play games?

Yes! We can arrange adventure activities like zip lining, paintballing, archery, horse riding and quad biking. We handle all transportation, logistics and activity coordination for an exciting day out.
