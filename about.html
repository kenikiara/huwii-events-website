The Team Behind the "Huwii!"

We're a passionate crew of creatives, planners and problem-solvers who love bringing ideas to life — and we've been making events unforgettable across Kenya for over seven years.

Our story

Founded to Make Every Event Extraordinary

Huwii Events started with a simple belief: people do their best work — and have their best moments — when they're genuinely having fun together. What began with a handful of giant games and a lot of energy has grown into Kenya's all-in-one events company.

Today we deliver team building experiences, interactive games for sale and hire, event equipment and complete event management for corporates, schools, church groups, NGOs and families. We're based in Nairobi, but we move the entire country — from Mombasa to Nakuru and everywhere in between.

  • Professional facilitators, MCs and event coordinators
  • Our own fleet of games and event equipment
  • Venue sourcing, transport and logistics handled end-to-end
  • Experiences tailored to your goals, group size and budget
